std::uint32_t Lightweight::SqlBackup::BackupSettings::level = 6

The compression level (0-9).

  • For Deflate: 1 = fastest, 9 = best compression, 6 = default
  • For Bzip2: 1-9 (block size in 100k units)
  • For Zstd: maps to zstd levels
  • For Store: ignored
